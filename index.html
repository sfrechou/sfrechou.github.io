Features

Performance

Centralize all your performance indicators and use our user friendly and dynamic analysis tools for a better exploration of your team's achievements and improvement possibilities.

Well-Being

Keep up with the well-being of your players by having them input their daily stats which you can instantly see and reference when needed.

Injury Registry

Operate a historic record all player injuries in detail to monitor and follow up as needed.

Compare

Use our dynamic charts to compare different players with different critera for a detailed performance analysis that will keep you ahead of the competition.

Server

Our servers will store all the historic records for you to reference whenever. The more access to your own information, the better you'll know your team.

Results

Ultimately watch your team grow and stay strong. Use technology to your advantage for your best results on the field.

Daily Well-Being Record

A practical way for players to input their daily values so that they are instantly available for analysis and ready before training sessions begin.

Dashboard

Access all functionalities through the customized dashboard for all staff members where you'll have all the tools easily available.

Player Profile

Centralize all player information and access it through their profiles. PlayerTrack's predictive model allows for injury risk analysis that can help you prevent predictable injuries and avoid unnecessary match absences. In each of their profiles you can also access historic match stats and their injury records for a better follow up when needed.

Compare Section

Use PlayerTrack's unique variables to analyze and compare all players for a detailed performance evaluation.

Go

Our common interest in sports and the drive to push ourselves inspired the idea for this project. We got in contact with the team who provided us with the GPS data and could talk first hand and see if there were any technological needs they had, something besides what they already have that could help with their performance. We found out we could not only create a project that would challenge us as programmers but also would solve a real problem and had the potential to be scaled not only to other team sports in other disciplines but also to other behavioral models.
We managed to finish on time by creating a careful plan for every section, sticking to the main idea of the MVP and making it fully functional. Having our research be done with real players' information gave us the motivation to continue given that what we were creating was useful and inexistant in this market.
Our plan is to continue pushing through with this project and pushing its potential to see where it leads us.
